SMRITI SRIVASTAVA







Cinema Journey







I have always been interested in cinema, and I wanted to explore different aspects of filmmaking. That’s why I decided to work in various projects, both in films and web series, with different directors, actors, and genres. I wanted to learn from the best, and also showcase my versatility and talent.

Ayushman Khurrana & Anubhuti Kashyap

One of the projects that I am very proud of is “Doctor G”, a comedy film directed by Anubhuti Kashyap and produced by Junglee Pictures. I got to work with some of the most talented and popular actors in the industry, such as Ayushman Khurrana and Rakulpreet Singh. I played the role of a medical student who gets involved in a hilarious situation with the titular doctor. The film was a lot of fun to shoot, and I enjoyed the chemistry and camaraderie with my co-stars. The film was also well-received by the audience and critics, and it was a box-office hit.

Tigmanshu Dhulia

Another project that I worked on was “Garmi”, a web series directed by Tigmanshu Dhulia and streamed on Zee5. I played the role of a journalist who investigates a series of murders in a small town during a heat wave. The web series was a thriller with a social message, and it dealt with some serious issues such as corruption, casteism, and violence. The web series was challenging and intense, and I got to learn a lot from the director and my co-actors. The web series was also praised for its gripping story and performances, and it won several awards.

Ruchir Arun

I am currently working on another web series called “The Lawyer’s Show”, directed by Ruchir Arun and streamed on Netflix. I play the role of a lawyer who fights for justice and human rights in various cases. The web series is a drama with a touch of humor, and it showcases the legal system and its loopholes. The web series is exciting and engaging, and I am having a great time working with the director and my co-actors. The web series is expected to release soon, and I hope that it will be appreciated by the viewers.

Naveen Mishra

The most recent project that I have done is “Mimansa”, a feature film directed by Naveen Mishra. I played the lead role of a woman who suffers from a rare psychological disorder that makes her see things that are not there. The film was a psychological thriller with a twist, and it explored the themes of identity, reality, and illusion. The film was very challenging and demanding, both physically and mentally, and I had to prepare extensively for my role. The film was also very rewarding and satisfying, as I got to portray a complex and layered character. The film is yet to release, but I am confident that it will be well-received by the audience.



These are some of the projects that I have worked on in my cinema experience so far. I am grateful for all the opportunities that I have got, and I always try to give my best in every project. I am passionate about cinema, and I will never stop learning and growing as an actress. This is my cinema experience, and I hope that you enjoyed reading it.
